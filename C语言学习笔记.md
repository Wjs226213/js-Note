# 不定积分练习题

## 一、基础公式类

1. \(\displaystyle \int x^2 \, dx\)
2. \(\displaystyle \int (3x^2 - 4x + 1)\, dx\)
3. \(\displaystyle \int e^x \, dx\)
4. \(\displaystyle \int \sin x \, dx\)
5. \(\displaystyle \int \cos 3x \, dx\)
6. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx\)
7. \(\displaystyle \int a^x \, dx \quad (a>0, a\neq 1)\)

## 二、换元积分法

8. \(\displaystyle \int 2x e^{x^2} \, dx\)
9. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{2x}{1+x^2} \, dx\)
10. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \, dx\)
11. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x\ln x} \, dx\)
12. \(\displaystyle \int \sqrt{1+x} \, dx\)
13. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{x}{\sqrt{1+x^2}} \, dx\)

## 三、分部积分法

14. \(\displaystyle \int x e^x \, dx\)
15. \(\displaystyle \int x \sin x \, dx\)
16. \(\displaystyle \int x \cos x \, dx\)
17. \(\displaystyle \int (\ln x)\, dx\)
18. \(\displaystyle \int x^2 e^x \, dx\)
19. \(\displaystyle \int x \ln x \, dx\)

## 四、含三角函数与有理函数

20. \(\displaystyle \int \tan x \, dx\)
21. \(\displaystyle \int \sec^2 x \, dx\)
22. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{1+x^2} \, dx\)
23. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{(1+x^2)^2} \, dx\)
24. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{\sin x}{1+\cos x} \, dx\)
25. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sin x \cos x} \, dx\)

## 五、综合一点的题

26. \(\displaystyle \int x^3 e^{x^2} \, dx\)
27. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{\ln x}{x} \, dx\)
28. \(\displaystyle \int x^2 \ln x \, dx\)
29. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{x}{(1-x)^2} \, dx\)
30. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{\sin x \cos x}{1+\sin^2 x} \, dx\)
